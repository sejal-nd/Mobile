Delmarva Power’s free app allows you to easily access your account information on the go. The app makes it simple to manage more than one account at a time and is available to both residential and business customers on your smartphone or tablet. You can report your outage and stay up-to-date on the restoration status from anywhere. Key features include: 

· Effortlessly view and pay your bill 
· Report and check outage status
· View the outage map 
· Check balance 
· View account activity 
· Manage your AutoPay and Budget Billing settings 
. Report an outage without signing in 
· View usage data, bill forecast and trends 
· Compare your bills 
· Set alerts for outage status, when your bill is ready, upcoming payment reminders, and more
